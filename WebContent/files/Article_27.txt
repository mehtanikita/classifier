Two tribal girls, who had been missing from their school hostel since last month, were found dead in a forest area on Sunday with their bodies chopped into pieces, police said.\r\nThe girls, identified as Bhoomika and Priyanka, both students of class IX at a government-run school for tribal children in Moodu Chekkalapally village, had been missing since November 23 from the hostel where they were staying, a police officer at Chennaraopeta police station said.\r\nTheir parents had filed missing complaints, based on which the Chennaraopeta police launched a search for them. On Sunday, some villagers went to a forest in Khadhar hills under Chennaraopeta mandal and called the police after they experienced a stench. The bodies were found and identified with the help of the girls\u0092 parents, officers said.
