New Delhi [India], Apr 17 (ANI): Get ready to witness the 'battle royale' this Diwali, as ' Golmaal Again' too confirms its release date during the festival of lights.\r\n\r\nBasically, when the makers announced the fourth installment of 'Golmaal' franchise, they mentioned that the film would release during Diwali 2017. But later things got uncertain when they delayed to start the film.\r\n\r\nNow when Ajay Devgn and Rohit Shetty confirmed 'GolmaalAgain's Diwali release, they have to fight Rajinikanth-Akshay Kumar starrer '2.0' and Aamir Khan produced ' Secret Superstar' at the Box Office.\r\n\r\nConfirming the news, film critic Taran Adarsh tweeted, \"It's OFFICIAL... #GolmaalAgain will release in Diwali 2017... Clash confirmed: #2point0, #SecretSuperstar and #GolmaalAgain.\"
